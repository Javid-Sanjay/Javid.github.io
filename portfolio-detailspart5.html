The Augmented Reality for Smart Education project aims to enhance traditional learning methods by leveraging augmented reality technology. This project utilizes AR to create an interactive educational experience where users can explore chemical bonding concepts through the merging of virtual cards representing different elements and can able to learn much more concepts.
