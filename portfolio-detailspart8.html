The series of video editing projects undertaken for college events such as "Waves," cultural programs, and chief guest welcoming ceremonies involved meticulous editing to produce captivating content for YouTube. Each video aimed to encapsulate the essence of the event, highlighting memorable moments, performances, speeches, and celebrations, and presenting them in a visually engaging and narrative-driven format.
