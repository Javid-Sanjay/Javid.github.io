Zombie Chase Adventure

Project information

  • Category: Game design
  • Project date: 2024

Zombie Chase Adventure is an immersive third-person adventure game developed in Unity. In this game, players control a fully animated character that explores a dynamic environment, collecting coins and avoiding enemies. The game features intelligent AI-driven enemies like zombies, which patrol specific areas and chase the player when they come within a certain radius. The player must strategically navigate the environment, managing health and avoiding deadly encounters with zombies and boss enemies.
